SQL Deep Dive: Identifying Operational Thresholds

This query identified the "breaking point" where warehouse utilization caused nonlinear spikes in shipment delays:


-- Identifying 'Critical' capacity statuses 
SELECT 
    date, region, warehouse_utilization_percent, 
    delay_hours_avg,
    CASE 
        WHEN warehouse_utilization_percent > 90 THEN 'Critical'
        ELSE 'Normal'
    END AS status
FROM logistics_performance
WHERE region = 'Asia-Pacific' AND delay_hours_avg > 4.0
ORDER BY warehouse_utilization_percent DESC;
Discovery: When utilization hit 91%, delays consistently spiked to 5.0 hours, triggering a direct increase in support volume.
