Global Logistics Intelligence & Predictive Pipeline

End-to-End Analytics: SQL ETL & Tableau Predictive Modeling

Interactive Dashboard View SQL Scripts

Business Challenge

Fragmented data across customer, shipment, and logistics datasets made it impossible for executive leadership to visualize the correlation between operational efficiency and the bottom line. The goal was to consolidate 56,000+ shipment records and 750 customer accounts into a predictive tool to manage an $11.36M revenue stream.

Technical Execution

  • Data Engineering: Used SQL (PostgreSQL) to perform complex joins and CTEs, transforming raw CSV data into a clean, relational schema.
  • BI Visualization: Authored Level of Detail (LOD) expressions in Tableau to calculate critical KPIs like Support Ticket Ratios and ROI.
  • Predictive Analytics: Built a 3-year forecasting model to project revenue and support demand through 2029.

Executive Summary Dashboard

The central dashboard provides a high-level view of $11.3M in revenue, with sparklines indicating a 17.2:1 ROI on acquisition spend.

Deep Dive: Identifying Operational Thresholds

Using the following SQL query, I identified a critical "breaking point" where warehouse utilization caused nonlinear spikes in shipment delays:


-- Identifying 'Critical' capacity statuses in Asia-Pacific
SELECT 
    date, 
    region, 
    warehouse_utilization_percent, 
    delay_hours_avg,
    CASE 
        WHEN warehouse_utilization_percent > 90 THEN 'Critical'
        ELSE 'Normal'
    END AS status
FROM logistics_performance
WHERE region = 'Asia-Pacific' 
AND delay_hours_avg > 4.0
ORDER BY warehouse_utilization_percent DESC;

Discovery: When utilization hit 91%, delays consistently spiked to 5.0 hours, leading to a direct increase in support volume.
